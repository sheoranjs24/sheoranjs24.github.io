About Me

I am a Software Development Engineer at AbeBooks Inc, an Amazon company. A programmer by heart, I love building things and solving problems. My hobbies are coding, photography, gardening, reading books and dancing. Checkout my public photostream at Flickr.

I completed my MSc. in Computer Science from University of Victoria in August 2015. Checkout my research publications at ACM Digital Library. Before joining the MSc. programme, I was working as an Application Engineer at the Amdocs Developer Centre, Pune, India. I hold a degree in Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Maharashi Dayanand University, Rohtak, India.
